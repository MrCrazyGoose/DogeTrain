How to play:

Move the doge around to collect as many dogecoins as possible, without hitting yourself or the edge of the canvas.

Controls:

Mobile:

Movement:

  • Drag Controls
  • Touch/Click Controls

Mystery Sound:

  • Click Doge Head

Computer:

Movement:

  • Drag Controls
  • Click controls
  • WASD
  • Arrow Keys

Mystery Sound:

  • Click Doge Head
  • Space Bar
