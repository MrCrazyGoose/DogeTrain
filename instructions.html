How to play:

Move the doge around to collect as many dogecoins as possible, without hitting yourself or the edge of the canvas. You can use the share button (shows up at the end of the game) to share your high score and a link to play with the same settings that you did.

Controls:

Mobile:

Movement:

  • Drag Controls
  • Touch/Click Controls

Mystery Sound:

  • Click Doge Head

Play Again:

  • Refresh
  • "Play Again" button (in the location of "Start Game" button)

Start Game:

  • "Start Game" button

Reset Settings to Default:

  • "Reset Settings" button

Computer:

Movement:

  • Drag Controls
  • Click controls
  • WASD
  • Arrow Keys

Mystery Sound:

  • Click Doge Head
  • Space Bar

Play Again:

  • Refresh
  • "Play Again" button (in the location of "Start Game" button)
  • Press any key after game

Start Game:

  • "Start Game" button
  • Press Alt-S

Reset Settings to Default:

  • "Reset Settings" button
  • Press Alt-R
